# How to Sign Up for Welco

This guide walks you through creating your Welco account, from visiting the signup page to accessing your dashboard for the first time. The entire process typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address that you can access immediately
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A payment method for after your free trial (credit card or PayPal)
- Basic information about your organization (if signing up for a team account)

### 1. Visit the Welco Signup Page

1. Navigate to `https://app.welco.ai/signup` in your web browser
2. You'll see the main signup form with options for individual and team accounts

üí° **Tip**: Bookmark this page if you're helping team members sign up later.

### 2. Fill Out Your Account Information

Complete the required fields in the signup form:

1. **Full Name**: Enter your first and last name
   - Example: `Sarah Johnson`
2. **Email Address**: Use a valid email you can access immediately
   - Example: `sarah.johnson@company.com`
3. **Password**: Create a strong password with at least 8 characters
   - Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
   - Example: `MySecure123!`
4. **Phone Number**: Add your phone number for account security
5. **Industry**: Select your Industry from the dropdown
6. Click **Create Account** to proceed

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important**: Use your primary work email address to ensure you receive all account notifications and can easily recover your account if needed.

### 4. Complete Your Profile Setup

![Profile settings](/images/profilesettings.png)

You can go to the profile completion page

1. **Time Zone**: Select your time zone from the dropdown
   - This ensures timestamps and scheduling features work correctly
2. Click **Save Changes** to continue

### 6. Start Your Free Trial

All new accounts include a 7-day free trial:

1. Review the trial details on the confirmation screen
2. Your trial includes access to all Pro features
3. No payment method required during the trial period
4. Click **Start Free Trial** to activate your account

üö® **Warning**: Your free trial will automatically expire after 7 days.

### 7. Access Your Welco Dashboard

After completing signup:

1. You'll be automatically logged into your Welco dashboard
2. Take a moment to explore the main navigation menu
3. Complete the optional onboarding tour to learn key features
4. Your account is now fully active and ready to use

### Password Requirements

**Issue**: Password doesn't meet requirements
**Solution**:

- Ensure at least 8 characters total
- Include at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Include at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- Include at least one number (0-9)
- Include at least one special character (!@#\$%^&\*)

### Account Creation Errors

**Issue**: "Email already exists" error
**Solution**:

- This email is already associated with a Welco account
- Try logging in instead of creating a new account
- Use the password reset option if you've forgotten your credentials
- Contact support if you believe this is an error
