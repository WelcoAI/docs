Welco supports real-time appointment scheduling by integrating directly with [Cal.com](http://Cal.com). Once connected, your AI receptionist can automatically schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments during live calls based on your availability.

⸻

## Step-by-Step: How to Connect Your [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) Calendar

### Step 1: Open the Calendar Integration Panel

	1.	Navigate to Configuration \> Real-Time Integrations.

	2.	Under Calendar Connection, click Configure Calendar.

⸻

### Step 2: Choose Calendar Type

	•	You will see options for Google, Outlook, and Apple Calendar.

	•	Select any option – all redirect to the same [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) integration process.

Click Next: Connect Calendar to continue.

⸻

### Step 3: Enter Your [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) API Key

	1.	Retrieve your API key from your [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) account.

View the official [Cal.com API key guide](https://cal.com/docs/api-reference/v1/authentication)

	2.	Paste the API key into the field provided.

	3.	Click Next: Select Event.

⸻

### Step 4: Configure Scheduling Settings

You will be guided to select:

	•	Which event type the AI should book (e.g., consultation, follow-up)

	•	Any custom availability rules

	•	Whether to allow automatic rescheduling or cancellation

Once completed, your calendar will be live and available to Welco’s AI during calls.

⸻

## How It Works in Real Time

Once connected:

	•	The AI receptionist can check your availability.

	•	It can offer open time slots to the caller.

	•	It will automatically create the booking on your behalf via [Cal.com](http://Cal.com).

⸻

## Best Practices

	•	Keep your [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) availability updated.

	•	Limit event types to only the ones the AI should access.

	•	Use intake workflows to collect caller info before scheduling.

	•	Monitor [Cal.com](http://Cal.com) for upcoming appointments and no-shows.
